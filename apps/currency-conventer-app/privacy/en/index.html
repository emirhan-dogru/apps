Currency Converter Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 06/05/2025

This privacy policy explains how the Currency Converter mobile application protects your privacy and handles data.

1. Data Collection

Currency Converter does not collect any personal data from its users. The application only retrieves exchange rate data via the https://frankfurter.dev/ API, and this data is temporarily processed on your device.

2. Data Sharing

Our application does not store or share user data with any third parties.

3. Data Security

Since no user data is collected, there are no additional data security measures required. However, we ensure that exchange rate data is sourced reliably through the https://frankfurter.dev/ API.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please send an email to emirhan-dogru@hotmail.com.

5. Changes and Updates

Currency Converter reserves the right to update this privacy policy. It is the user’s responsibility to regularly check for updates.
