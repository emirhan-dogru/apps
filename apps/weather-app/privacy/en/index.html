Live - Weather Forecast App Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Live - Weather Forecast mobile application collects, uses, protects, and discloses the services provided by EDR Software Corp. to preserve the privacy of its users.

Information Collected

The Live - Weather Forecast application may collect user location information and access the device's location services. This information is used to provide the user with local weather information and to improve service quality. Additionally, anonymous data such as usage statistics and device information may be collected to enhance the user experience and optimize our services.

Usage of Information

Our application does not store any user information in any database or collect personal information from users.

Data Security

Since the Live - Weather Forecast application does not collect user data, there are no data security concerns.

Children's Privacy

The Live - Weather Forecast application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you become aware of any personal information of a child under 13, please contact us to promptly delete such information.

Changes and Updates

EDR Software Corp. reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time. In the event of any changes, the updated policy will be published within the application to inform users.
